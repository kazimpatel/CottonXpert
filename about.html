ABOUT US

Cotton Xpert plays a pivotal role in connecting buyers and suppliers, focusing primarily on the European market. As reliable and seasoned textile buying agents, we cater to the diverse needs of clients within the industry. With an impressive 8-year track record in Pakistan's textile sector, Cotton Xpert possesses a profound understanding of the market. Our extensive network of contacts within Pakistan's textile industry, combined with our robust product knowledge and technical expertise, ensures accurate fulfillment of customer orders. Operating from Karachi, Pakistan, Cotton Xpert provides a comprehensive range of services, including product design, development, sourcing, factory selection, merchandising, manufacturing control, shipping consolidation, and inventory monitoring for N.O.O.S. programs..

OUR HISTORY

Founded in December 2016, Cotton Xpert Textile emerged as a prominent Textile Buying Agent operating in Pakistan. Leveraging its dedicated and skilled teams, Cotton Xpert has successfully catered to a diverse clientele, including mail order/catalogue houses, department stores, retail outlets, and importers on a global scale. Over the years, the company has established a strong presence in regions spanning the Mediterranean countries, the Far East, the Middle East, and Europe. Committed to professionalism and excellence, Cotton Xpert continues to play a crucial role in facilitating seamless textile transactions and fostering international partnerships.

Supplier Sourcing


Cotton Xpert offers its services to their foreign principals, representing them as their buying agent in Pakistan. We work with Pakistani suppliers who are from major industrial groups with vertical setups, well equipped, having a strong financial base, located in Karachi, Lahore, Faisalabad and Multan; these suppliers are selected through our vigorous evaluation process. we constantly assess the manufacturing and managment capabilities of suppliers by evaluation by plant, machinery and equipment, as well as labour skills, social compliance. financial strength and management style of them company.

Social Compliance


As per buyers requirement, Cotton Xpert audits its supplier's facilities to ensure they pass their social compliance policy which includes:

  • Health and Safety procedures.
  • Zero tolerance for child labour/ prison labour/ forced labour.
  • Provision of required standards for working, eating and sanitation facilities.
  • Minimum wages as per the country's norms.
  • Working Hours to conform to country's labour laws.

Logistic Support


Our experienced logistics department works dilligently and communicates with the buyers import department and their forwarding agen for timely shipments which includes checkking/tracking shipping documents, freight, consolidation and cordination of information between all the parties involved. The cooperation results in unnecessary delays and demurrage charges.
